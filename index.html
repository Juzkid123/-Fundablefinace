// About the Project

What is FundableHQ?

FundableHQ is a Web3 fundraising intelligence platform that helps blockchain startups raise capital the smart way — connecting founders with active investors, polishing pitch narratives, and facilitating warm introductions to VCs, launchpads, and CEXs.

In a market where cold emails are ignored and ghosted decks are the norm, FundableHQ builds structured fundraising campaigns and positions projects in front of vetted deal-flow communities — delivering 25+ targeted investor introductions per startup.

Operating at the intersection of DeFi, VC fundraising, and community-led growth, FundableHQ is redefining how Web3 startups go from idea to raise.
